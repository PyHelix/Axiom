    FINDING: Soft orthogonality penalty modestly rescues compositionality.
    ortho_rescues_compositionality=true consistently across seeds.
    ortho_helps_wide_networks is MIXED (true in some, false in others).
    Effect is real but modest compared to bottleneck (Finding #37).

37. BOTTLENECK COMPOSITIONALITY — DEFINITIVELY CONFIRMED (~230+ seeds, 93.4%)
    FINDING: Bottleneck layer [W->B->W] STRONGLY rescues compositional generalization.
    bottleneck_helps_compositionality=true in 171/183 keyed results (93.4%).
    Gap reduction: 4-8% absolute (vs 1-2% for ortho, 0% for pruning).
    Best configs: w128_b16 (gap ~0.57 vs baseline ~0.94), w256_b32 (gap ~0.55).
    FIRST INTERVENTION THAT REVERSES WIDTH-COMPOSITIONALITY TRADEOFF.
    Hierarchy: bottleneck >> ortho >> dropout >> pruning(none).

38. COMBINED COMPOSITIONALITY — GROWING, APPROX. ADDITIVE (13 seeds)
    FINDING: Bottleneck + ortho combined shows APPROXIMATELY ADDITIVE effect.
    NOT synergistic — combined ≈ sum of individual effects.
    Synergy detected: 10/13 True, but mean gap synergy only +0.003 (≈0).
    Effect types: approx_additive=7, superadditive=4, subadditive=2.
    Best intervention: "both" in 5/13, "none" in 4, "bottleneck_only" in 3.
    Bottleneck remains the dominant component. Ortho adds small marginal benefit.
    Needs more seeds for statistical power — deployed at weight 3.
