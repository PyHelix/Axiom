36. ORTHOGONALITY COMPOSITIONALITY — GROWING CONFIRMATION (75 seeds)
    FINDING: Soft orthogonality penalty modestly rescues compositionality.
    ortho_rescues_compositionality=true consistently across seeds.
    ortho_helps_wide_networks is MIXED (true in some, false in others).
    Baseline gaps confirm Finding #31. Ortho gives ~1-2% gap improvement per width.
    Effect is real but modest compared to bottleneck (Finding #37).

37. BOTTLENECK COMPOSITIONALITY — STRONG POSITIVE SIGNAL (31 seeds)
    FINDING: Bottleneck layer [W→B→W] STRONGLY rescues compositional generalization.
    bottleneck_helps_compositionality=true in ALL sampled seeds.
    Gap reduction: 4-8% absolute (vs 1-2% for ortho, 0% for pruning).
    Best configs: w128_b16 (gap ~0.57 vs baseline ~0.94), w256_b32 (gap ~0.55).
    FIRST INTERVENTION THAT REVERSES WIDTH-COMPOSITIONALITY TRADEOFF (Finding #31).
    Bottleneck rank stays well below width — confirms architectural constraint works.
    Hierarchy of interventions: bottleneck >> ortho >> dropout >> pruning(none).
