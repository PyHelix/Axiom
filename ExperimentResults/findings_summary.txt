33. NEURON SPECIALIZATION — CONFIRMED (~1160+ results)
    FINDING: Wider nets produce MORE selective neurons (selectivity increases
    0.654→0.670) but with POOR feature-group alignment (0.364→0.343).
    Effective dim ratio PLUMMETS: 0.281→0.071 from W32→W256.

34. PRUNING COMPOSITIONALITY — DEFINITIVELY CONFIRMED NEGATIVE & RETIRED (48 seeds)
    FINDING: Magnitude pruning does NOT recover compositional generalization.

35. REGULARIZED COMPOSITIONALITY — STRONGLY CONFIRMED (~2400+ results)
    FINDING: Weight decay (0.05) reduces compositional gap at ALL widths.
    Dropout shows asymmetric width interaction: helps narrow not wide.
    Best reg: dropout_0.4 for W32, wd_0.05 for W64-W256 (100% dominant).

36. ORTHOGONALITY COMPOSITIONALITY — STRONGLY CONFIRMED (~1900+ results)
    FINDING: Soft orthogonality penalty rescues compositionality (100% True).
    ortho_helps_wide_networks: ~71% True. Effect modest vs bottleneck.

37. BOTTLENECK COMPOSITIONALITY — DEFINITIVELY CONFIRMED (~1800+ results, 98%)
    FINDING: Bottleneck layer [W->B->W] STRONGLY rescues compositionality.
    Gap reduction: 4-8% absolute. Best: w128_b16, w256_b32.
    Hierarchy: bottleneck >> ortho >> dropout >> pruning(none).

38. COMBINED COMPOSITIONALITY — GROWING (~1750+ results)
    FINDING: Combining bottleneck+ortho is usually beneficial (75.8%).
    Synergy detected ~82%. Effect types variable: superadditive/subadditive/additive.
    Bottleneck remains the dominant component.

39. BOTTLENECK MECHANISM — PRELIMINARY (3 results, 1 effective seed)
    Bottleneck reduces comp gap at W32/W64/W128 (not W16).
    Gap reductions: W16→-0.015, W32→+0.110, W64→+0.140, W128→+0.085.
    Reduces feature redundancy at 75% of widths.
    Does NOT increase effective rank (0%) — mechanism is information compression
    through narrow channel, not capacity expansion. Needs independent seeds.
    Script: bottleneck_mechanism.py. ISSUE: seed=42 on all hosts.

40. REPRESENTATION DISENTANGLEMENT — HYPOTHESIS PARTIALLY REFUTED (86 seeds)
    KEY FINDING: Wider networks do NOT have worse disentanglement!
    DCI disentanglement: W32=0.139, W64=0.135, W128=0.146, W256=0.151 (flat/up).
    MI disentanglement: 0.586→0.593 (stable).
    But comp gap still rises: 0.858→0.878 (confirms Finding #31).
    Effective rank ratio plummets: 0.498→0.075 (confirms Finding #33).
    CONCLUSION: Width-compositionality tradeoff driven by rank COLLAPSE,
    not entanglement. Disentanglement is NOT the mechanism.

41. COMPOSITIONALITY CRITICAL PERIOD — NEW (deployed s0302cc, 0 results yet)
    HYPOTHESIS: There is a critical training epoch where compositional
