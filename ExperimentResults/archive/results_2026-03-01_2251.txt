NEW EXPERIMENT: grokking_dynamics_v5.py — DESIGN RATIONALE
============================================================

MOTIVATION:
  v4 FAILED on two fronts: (1) broken seeding producing identical
  replications, and (2) insufficient model capacity for weight decay
  to create the compression dynamics needed for grokking.

ROOT CAUSE ANALYSIS:
  v4: P=23, hidden=64, wd=1.0 → weight norm peaked at 51.9,
  declined only 4% to 49.8. Not enough compression.

  v2: P=97, hidden=128, wd=1.0 → weight norm peaked at 157.5,
  declined 12% to 138.0. Grokking in progress (49% test at 100K).

  The key metric is WEIGHT NORM DECLINE RATE. v2's 12% decline
  drove test accuracy from 0% to 49%. v4's 4% decline produced
  nothing. The larger model (hidden=128) creates a higher weight
  norm that gives weight decay more "material" to compress.

v5 CHANGES:
  1. SEEDING FIX: Three-strategy seed resolution:
     - Primary: Read wu.json directly (BOINC open_name)
     - Secondary: Scan all .json files in cwd and parent dir
     - Fallback: hostname + PID + timestamp (unique but not reproducible)
     Debug info recorded in output for diagnosis.

  2. hidden_dim=128 (back to v2's size):
     More parameters → higher weight norm → stronger compression signal.
     Expected weight norm peak: ~150-160 (matching v2).

  3. weight_decay=3.0 (3x v2/v3/v4):
     Stronger compression pressure to overcome the memorization basin.
     With higher wd, the effective step includes: param * (1 - lr * wd)
     At wd=3.0, lr=0.001: per-step decay = 0.997 vs 0.999 at wd=1.0.
     Over 100K epochs: cumulative decay = 0.997^100K ≈ 0 vs 0.999^100K ≈ 0.

  4. GELU activation (replaces ReLU):
     Smoother gradients than ReLU. The grokking literature (Power et al.)
     uses GELU. Smoother activation may help find flatter minima that
     generalize better. GELU approximation: 0.5*x*(1+tanh(sqrt(2/pi)*(x+0.044715*x^3)))

  5. P=23, lr=0.001, 500K epochs (same as v4):
     Smallest useful prime. lr=0.001 showed progress in v2.

PREDICTION:
  With hidden=128 and wd=3.0, weight norm should peak at ~150 and
  decline ~20-30% over 500K epochs (much stronger than v4's 4%).
  If the compression is strong enough, test accuracy should start
  climbing around epoch 50K-200K. GELU may additionally help.

  If v5 still fails, the next step would be to try SGD with momentum
  (instead of AdamW) as some papers show SGD groks more reliably.
